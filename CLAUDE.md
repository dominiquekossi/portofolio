# Copy rules

- Never use the em-dash character (—) in any visible site text. Use a comma, a period, or
  restructure the sentence instead. (Code comments are not "visible site text" and are exempt.)
- Never name specific employers or clients in site copy. Describe the type of work generically
  instead (e.g. "an AI-focused startup" rather than the company name). Recreated-project
  credits are the exception and may be named (e.g. Zentry, Build in Amsterdam) since they are
  design attribution, not employer names.
- Fixed accent color: `#a9752c` (ochre gold). Do not introduce blue, or any gradient, as the
  site accent.
